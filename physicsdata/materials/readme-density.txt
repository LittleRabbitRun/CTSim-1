All density_*.txt consist of one single number, the
element's or compound's denisty in g/cm^3.

The data that was obtained from
http://physics.nist.gov/PhysRefData/XrayMassCoef/tab1.html
and
http://physics.nist.gov/PhysRefData/XrayMassCoef/tab2.html
between 2011-02-14 and 2011-08-22.

Materials that are preceeded by "AK-" denote materials
whose properties have been computed by Andreas Keil.
